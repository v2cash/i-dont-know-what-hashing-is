Why viper128?

viper128 is not like any other encryption algorithm, here is why:
  1. We support all Character encoding including from ISO 646 to ISO 8859-16
  2. WordWrap is fully supported
  3. ASCII Art is fully supported
  4. All characters that support its native format are fully supported
  5. With a secure key and a big strength pattern, it will take a desktop computer over a year to crack your encryption.
  6. Encrypt Large or small strings without a issue
